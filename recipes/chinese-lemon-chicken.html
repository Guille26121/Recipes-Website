Lemon chicken is the name of several dishes found in cuisines around the world which include chicken and lemon. In Canadian- and British-Chinese cuisine, it usually consists of pieces of chicken meat that are saut√©ed or battered and deep-fried and coated with a thick, sweet lemon-flavored sauce. Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • Sunflower oil
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • chicken soup
  • soy sauce
